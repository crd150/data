houstondems.shp: Houston Metropolitan Area
 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Data Dictionary
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 
GEOID: Census Tract ID


tpop: Total population - tract

pblk: Percent non-Hispanic black - tract

pwht: Percent non-Hispanic white - tract

pasn: Percent non-Hispanic Asian - tract

phisp: Percent Hispanic - tract

NAME: Metropolitan area name
