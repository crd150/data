--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Codebook for Assignment 6 data file oak_tracts
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 
All data were downloaded from the 2012-2016 American Community Survey 
Units of observations: Census tracts in 2010 boundaries
Oakland City boundaries.
 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Data Dictionary
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 
GEOID: Census Tract ID

tpop: Total population

pnhwhite: Percent of residents - non-Hispanic white

pnhasn: Percent of residents - non-Hispanic black

pnhblk: Percent of residents - non-Hispanic Asian

phisp: Percent of residents - Hispanic

medincome: Median household income

hunits: Total number of housing units

rent: Median gross rent

pvac: Percent of housing units that are vacant
